How it works

Four things.
In the right order.

No grammar. No courses. No levels. Just the words you need, reviewed at the right time, with the right friction.

01
Frequency-first
Words introduced in strict frequency order. The top 3,000 cover 95% of conversation. No filler, ever.
02
Retrieve before reveal
Spanish word shown first. You try to recall before the answer appears. The struggle is the learning.
03
Spaced repetition
SM-2 schedules reviews at the exact interval that maximises retention. Hard words sooner. Known words later.
04
Sleep & protect
Review before bed. Protect your streak. Sleep consolidates for free. Minimum session: 5 cards.
